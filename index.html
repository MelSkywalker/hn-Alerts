HN Alerts?

Technology is in constant evolution and every person involved in IT jobs must be aware of all the changes in the tech world.

But it evolves so quickly that it might be difficult to keep an eye on all these changes.

That is the problem HN Alerts solves.

Every half an hour HN Alerts looks for the latest Google and Facebook related news and tweets them, so users can activate HN Alerts notifications and be up to date in this sector of the tech world.

Do you want to stay up to date with technology?

Step 1:

Go to HN Google Alerts and HN Facebook Alerts.

Step 2:

Click the Follow button.

Step 3:

Activate HN Alerts notifications!


And that's it! You're ready to stay updated!
